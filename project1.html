Xandr Support & Optimization

Overview

This project highlights my role as an Analyst at Aceloution Inc, where I supported Microsoft's Invest & Monetize platform (formerly Xandr). I specialized in client-side technical support, data investigation, and campaign performance optimization.

Responsibilities

  • Resolved over 450 support tickets with a 98% SLA compliance rate.
  • Collaborated with external SSPs/DSPs and internal teams to debug delivery issues.
  • Utilized SQL and log-level data for in-depth analysis and root cause identification.
  • Monitored tag implementation, cookie syncing, and creative troubleshooting.
  • Provided actionable insights to improve delivery and fill rates for programmatic campaigns.
