Supporting the delivery and performance of programmatic advertising campaigns through technical troubleshooting, data analysis, and cross-functional collaboration.

Overview

Actively supported the delivery and performance of programmatic advertising campaigns by troubleshooting and resolving technical issues on the Xandr Invest and Monetize platforms. Ensured seamless integration and communication between buyers, sellers, and third-party data partners.

Core Responsibilities

  • Managed and resolved 450+ support tickets with 98% SLA adherence
  • Investigated data discrepancies and delivery issues across line items and deals
  • Facilitated troubleshooting for delivery delays, pacing issues, and spend behavior
  • Conducted debug auctions to trace root causes of auction loss or low bid rates
  • Analyzed log-level data to identify patterns affecting targeting performance
  • Ensured proper PMP deal configuration between buyers and publishers

Key Achievements

  • Improved resolution speed through pattern-based troubleshooting approaches
  • Reduced repeated escalations with client-friendly documentation
  • Managed complex troubleshooting across multiple stakeholders
  • Gained deep expertise in RTB, auction mechanics, and deal ID functioning
  • Developed cross-functional communication skills across advertisers and publishers
