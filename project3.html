Employee Attrition
Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Excel dashboard exploring patterns behind employee exits — designed for HR decision-makers with slicer-driven KPIs and multi-chart visualizations.

Overview

Designed and developed an Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard using Excel to explore patterns behind employee exits. The project focuses on identifying key drivers of attrition, analyzing workforce distribution, and providing actionable insights for HR decision-making.

Key Performance Indicators

Total Headcount
Avg Job Satisfaction
Work-Life Balance
Average Salary
Average Tenure

Objectives

  • Identify key reasons behind employee attrition
  • Analyze workforce distribution across departments and roles
  • Highlight KPIs: job satisfaction, work-life balance, salary, tenure
  • Provide interactive slicer-based filtering for HR decision-making

Impact & Takeaways

  • Identified hidden attrition patterns across roles and departments
  • Strengthened HR analytics and Excel dashboard design skills
  • Built an interactive attrition explorer with department-level drill-down
  • Practiced data storytelling for non-technical stakeholders
