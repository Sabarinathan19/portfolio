Xandr Invest & Monetize

Overview

Actively supported the delivery and performance of programmatic advertising campaigns by troubleshooting and resolving technical issues on the Xandr Invest and Monetize platforms. Ensured seamless integration and communication between buyers, sellers, and third-party data partners.

Core Responsibilities

  • Managed and resolved 450+ support tickets with a 98% SLA adherence, ensuring minimal disruption to campaign performance.
  • Investigated data discrepancies and delivery issues across line items, creatives, and deals.
  • Facilitated campaign troubleshooting for delivery delays, pacing issues, and unexpected spend behavior.
  • Conducted debug auctions to trace the root causes of auction loss, low bid rates, or invalid responses.
  • Analyzed log-level data to identify patterns affecting performance and targeting.
  • Collaborated with internal product teams and external clients to streamline ad delivery and deal setup.
  • Ensured proper deal configuration between buyers and publishers, including PMP (Private Marketplace) troubleshooting.

Tools & Techniques Used

  • Xandr UI (Invest & Monetize platforms)
  • Dbeaver / Postman
  • Salesforce / JIRA / UCMT
  • Excel for data slicing and customer-ready analysis

Key Achievements

  • Improved resolution speed through pattern-based troubleshooting approaches.
  • Reduced repeated escalations by implementing client-friendly documentation and proactive support practices.
  • Successfully managed complex troubleshooting across multiple stakeholders (advertisers, publishers, and data providers).

Impact & Takeaways

  • Strengthened ability to debug live ad delivery issues under time-sensitive conditions.
  • Gained deep understanding of RTB (Real-Time Bidding), auction mechanics, and deal ID functioning.
  • Developed client-facing communication skills and cross-functional collaboration experience.
